All controllers can have any number of (sub)controllers that they store in a dictionary in their

controllers
instVar.

The protocol to access a subcontroller is

controllerAt:

For example, you can..

Inspect this controller

and in the workspace area of the ispector type:

self controllerAt: #subexample
and press printIt (or inspect it) and you will see that it returns the corresponding subcontroller
